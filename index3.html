List of Supplies



  • Food: If you acquire a cat from a breeder, more often than not, their contract will either strongly suggest or require you to feed a certain kind of food. Canned foods are better for cats, but your cat may be used to dry food only. Grown cats thrive on dry food, and any of the premium brands are acceptable. You should also supplement with canned food because eventually, you should try to move your cat over to canned only.

  • Toys: Cats love to play, and toys are a great way to keep them entertained.

  • Scratching post: Cats naturally need to scratch to keep their claws sharp, and providing them with a scratching post will help protect your furniture.

  • Litter box and litter: A litter box and litter are essential for indoor cats. You should have one litter box per cat. The litter box should be large enough for your cat to turn around in, and it should be cleaned daily. There are many different types of litter available, including clay, clumping, and natural. You should choose a litter that your cat likes.

  • Brush: A brush is essential for removing loose hair and preventing hairballs. There are many different types of brushes available, including slicker brushes, bristle brushes, and combs.

  • Bed: Cats love to sleep, and providing them with a comfortable bed will help them feel safe and secure. There are many different types of beds available, including cat trees, cat condos, and simple cat beds.
